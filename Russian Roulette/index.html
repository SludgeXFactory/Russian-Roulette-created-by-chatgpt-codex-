How To Play

Turn Flow

Use items, aim at yourself or your opponent, confirm the shot, then let the turn auto-pass unless a fake self-shot keeps your turn alive.

Self Shots

Risk For Tempo

If you shoot yourself and the chamber is fake, you immediately keep control and may act again. If it is real, you eat the damage.
